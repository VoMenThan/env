Couchsurfing is a world-wide community of 14 million people in more than 200,000 cities who share their life, their world, their journey, and their experience. Couchsurfing connects travelers with a global network of people willing to share in profound and meaningful ways, making travel a truly social experience. The team envision a world made better by travel and travel made richer by connection. Couchsurfers share their lives with the people they encounter, fostering cultural exchange and mutual respect.
